Mark Zuckerberg

Biography

Mark Zuckerberg is an American computer programmer and internet entrepreneur who co-founded Facebook, Inc.

Education

Mark Zuckerberg attended Harvard University, where he studied computer science and psychology. He began programming at a young age and created several software programs during his time at Harvard. He famously launched Facebook from his dorm room in 2004.

Work

After launching Facebook, Zuckerberg dropped out of Harvard to focus on the platform, which rapidly gained popularity. Under his leadership, Facebook evolved from a college networking site to a global social media giant, reaching billions of users worldwide.

Achievements

  • Co-founder of Facebook, which has become one of the largest social media platforms.
  • Time magazine's Person of the Year (2010).
  • Named among the world's most influential people by various publications.
  • Philanthropy through the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative, focusing on education and health.
